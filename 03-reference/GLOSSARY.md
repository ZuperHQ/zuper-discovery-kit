# Plain-language glossary (optional reading)

| When we say | In Zuper it becomes |
|---|---|
| The kinds of work you do (roofing, gutters…) | **Trade types / business units** |
| A piece of work for a customer | A **job** |
| The kind of job (retail repair vs. insurance claim…) | A **job category** |
| The stages a job moves through | The **status pipeline** (each stage = a status) |
| The details you track on a job (claim #, carrier, system size…) | **Custom fields** |
| The form your crew fills at a stage (photos, readings, sign-offs) | A **checklist** |
| The paperwork you send (quote, invoice, contract, completion cert) | **Document templates** |
| Software you keep that should talk to Zuper | An **integration** |
| The numbers you need Monday morning | **Reports / dashboard** |
| The example jobs pre-loaded in your trial | **Seed data** |

You never need these words — answer in yours, the kit translates.
